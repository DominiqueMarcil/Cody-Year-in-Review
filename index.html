THE LAST YEAR

MY GROWTH THROUGH 2023

In November 2022, I took a leap by reaching out to the Lethbridge Hurricanes photographer, igniting a passion that has propelled my photography and videography journey to incredible heights over the past year. Since that momentous decision, I have experienced remarkable professional growth, capturing the electrifying moments of sporting events like the Calgary Stampede and FIS World Cup, CSEC, CEBL, the energy of concerts, and the behind-the-scenes magic of spec commercials for Mercedes & NIKE. My lens has not only witnessed grand spectacles but has also spotlighted the stories of local businesses. I am very appreciative of all the people who have helped me along the way. While challenges tested my skills and confidence, they only fueled my creative drive, and I'm eager to continue pushing boundaries, aiming for greater heights, and creating unforgettable memories through my work. The journey has just begun, and I'm excited to see where the future leads in the dynamic world of photography and videography.

OPPORTUNITIES

WESTERN HOCKEY LEAGUE

The WHL was my first “professional” job, igniting my journey in photography and videography. It was the spark that set the course for my passion of capturing moments and taking things more seriously.

CALGARY STAMPEDE

MY FIRST COMMERCIAL!!! Photographing the Calgary Stampede's rodeo and midway events was a thrilling adventure, it’s the greatest outdoor show in the world. It culminated in my first commercial feature on Sportsnet, marking a significant milestone.

CEBL

Joining the CEBL's Calgary Surge basketball team for their inaugural season was a pleasure. It allowed me to showcase my skills in a new environment, capturing the excitement of a brand-new venture in sports.

FIS WORLD CUP

Shooting the FIS Snowboard World Cup was pure exhilaration. It's been the most enjoyable experience behind the camera, getting to work face to face with the athletes. I had the opportunity to make a video with one of the Japanese riders getting featured on Burton Japan.

CSEC

Welcome to the “big leagues.” This fall I was very grateful to be hired by CSEC. Including the Calgary Flames, Wranglers, Hitmen, Roughnecks, and Stampeders. This requires me to be back in Calgary every weekend which is quite stressful but a key stepping stone.

MERCEDES

Shooting at Beama Studios for Mercedes spec work has been a unique opportunity, allowing me to gain true experience of being on a professional set and showcase the elegance of Mercedes-Benz.

CONCERTS

Concert photography is always a treat to be so close to the action. I've had the privilege of capturing unforgettable moments for bands like Mt. Joy, Dope Lemon, TALK, and local artists.

LOCAL BUSINESSES

Collaborating with local brands like ZENO Direct, Battle River Cannabis, WEST Clothing Co, and RebelX has been a rewarding experience. It has allowed me to create retainer clients which provide me work monthly. It is great getting to work with members in the community because it allows for us to grow together.
